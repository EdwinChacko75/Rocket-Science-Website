Reusable Rockets
Remember that guy we mentioned in the beginning, Sir Isaac Newton or something like that. Well that guy once said “what goes up must come down”. SpaceX has applied this idea to their Falcon 9 rocket by not simply sending it up, but by bringing the booster back down in a controlled manner. This drastically reduces the cost for space travel as the multi million dollar rocket can be reused rather than crashing into the ocean.
Fun Fact!
The Falcon 9 rocket uses JavaScript, HTML, and CSS in their displays!
